Operational notes The tracker status field is operational and may lag detailed review notes by up to one working day. The field Ready to Join indicates business onboarding readiness and is not equivalent to BPSS closure. Where the tracker and adjudication register conflict, the adjudication register is the authority for exception approval. Closure statuses Clear: all mandatory checks complete and no open material risk. Pending: one or more mandatory checks incomplete. Risk Accepted: exception approved by designated approvers; case may still remain operationally open if evidence collection continues. Reject: material issue found or evidence invalid.
